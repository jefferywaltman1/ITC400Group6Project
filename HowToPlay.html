THE RULES OF DEVIL'S LUCK
Devil's Luck is a game that consists of up to 3 rounds, and is best 2 out of 3. A player wins a round by having the most points on the board at the end of a round. A player wins by winning 2 rounds.
EACH ROUND CONSISTS OF TWO STEPS: SETUP AND COMBAT
